Narrative

. Hugh was from C.Antrim Belfast came to Australia" EllenStuart" Dec.1858 at 21 years of age. On his Death Certificate he was listed as retired Engineer. He died at 95 Myrtle St. Bendigo. Buried Bendigo Cemetery. He was a parishoner of St.AndrewsPresbyterian Church.died at 91 yr.old. Mother and Father not Known. 6 Living children. I, Janette Hadlow Attard spent holidays at 95 Myrtle St. with my grandmother Mary Agnes Mc.Shane Walsh The last time around 1848. The house was very close to a gold mine at Golden Square. There were lots of Mc.Shane relatives in Bendigo area.
