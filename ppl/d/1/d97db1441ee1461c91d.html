Narrative

Alexander came to Australia in January 1853, with Jessie Stewart his wife on the ship "Ben Nevis:" with children Catherine & Duncan (twins) 9 yrs.Jessie K .7 yrs. Benjamin 3 years . Anne 5 yrs. William 1 yr. All these children born Scotland. Isabella born 1856 Australia (Vic ?)Alexander born 1861 Aust. John born 1865.Fitzroy9Birth Cert.#1564) There is a Mary(Dec) and a Georgina born 1852 in Edinburgh who
died by 1851. The 1851 Edinburgh Census (Bo36.Pa7.Sc37) Midlothian lists Alexanders occupation then as Stamper at the Stamp Office and living att St.Johns St(172 Canongate) near ST.Margarets Chapel is between Arthurs Seat &Carlton Hill. Alexander listed Port Phillip Directory. B?Brooke (Fiche 53) at 116 Argyle St. His will( Series 12 Rec.no.351)Probate granted to Jessie Kelly 23/10/1874 He was Hotel Keeper of Volunteer Arms Hotel Fitzroy.when he died.
